Our Story

Gateway Promotions was established in 2013 as a small Wine and Liquor sampling team based out of Albany, New York. Our goal was to provide high quality samplers to local Albany stores. Since then, we expanded south to Kinderhook and all the way north to Lake George! In 2016, we added a Craft Beer team and a Promtional Modeling team. In 2017, our promotional events doubled. This lead to an increase in staff and a large increase in profit. In 2018, our goal is to continue to provide top of the line service to our customers and remain focused on growing Gateway Promotions.
