Our Story

Gateway Promotions was established in 2013 as a small Wine and Liquor sampling team based out of Albany, New York. Our goal was to provide high quality samplers to local Albany stores. We have since expanded south to Kinderhook and all the way North to Lake George! In 2016, we expanded our company to include a Craft Beer team and a Promtional Modeling team. Now, in 2017 we our looking to expand and add promotional customers whenever possible.
